## SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM

**Private fund managers are running their capital raise on a duct-tape stack.**

A typical $200M fund raise involves:
- Salesforce (CRM) — not built for LP relationships
- DocSend (data room) — no workflow integration
- Excel (commitment tracking) — manual, error-prone
- Email (LP communications) — no tracking or accountability
- DocuSign (subscriptions) — standalone, no context
- Fund admin software — siloed back office

**The result:**
- IR teams spend 40-50% of their time on admin, not relationship building
- Average fund raise takes 18 months — most of that is process friction
- LPs have no unified view of their alternative investments
- Capital matching is inefficient — the right LPs never find the right funds

**$30 trillion** in private market AUM. Running on spreadsheets and email.

## SLIDE 4: THE SOLUTION

**RaiseOS: The two-sided operating system for private capital.**

Like Shopify, we serve both sides of the market:

### For Fund Managers (GPs) — The Storefront
- Launch a branded fund page in minutes
- Manage every LP relationship in one AI-native CRM
- Automate DDQ responses, quarterly reports, capital call notices
- Track every investor's journey from prospect to committed capital
- AI tells you exactly what to do every day to close your fund faster

### For Investors (LPs) — The Shopping Experience
- Discover funds matched to your mandate in one curated marketplace
- Publish your investment mandate — let managers come to you
- Reverse bidding: qualified managers pitch for your capital
- One-click optimized checkout: KYC once, invest anywhere on platform
- Portfolio dashboard: all your alternatives in one place

**The network effect:** More GPs → better fund selection for LPs → more LP capital → more GPs want on platform → repeat.
