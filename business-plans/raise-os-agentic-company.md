# RaiseOS — Agentic Company Structure
## How This Company Runs Itself on AI Agents

---

## The Vision
RaiseOS is not just an AI-native product — it's an AI-native company.
Small human core (3-5 people) orchestrating a fleet of specialized AI agents.
Every function that can be automated, is automated.
Humans do: strategy, relationships, taste, judgment.
Agents do: everything else.

## The Agent Fleet

### 🛠️ Product & Engineering Agents
- **Codex/Claude Code Agent** — writes code 24/7. Takes tickets from Linear, ships PRs, opens for human review
- **QA Agent** — tests every PR automatically before human review
- **Bug Triage Agent** — monitors error logs, classifies bugs by severity, auto-creates tickets
- **Docs Agent** — keeps product documentation up to date as code changes

### 📈 Sales & Marketing Agents
- **Prospect Research Agent** — monitors LinkedIn, conference lists, news for new GP/LP prospects. Enriches CRM automatically
- **Outreach Agent** — drafts personalized cold outreach for human review and approval before sending
- **Content Agent** — monitors AI/fintech/private markets trends, drafts LinkedIn posts and newsletter content for Mark to review
- **SEO Agent** — monitors keyword rankings, suggests content improvements

### 🎧 Customer Success Agents
- **Onboarding Agent** — guides new GP customers through setup via automated email sequences + in-app flows
- **Support Agent** — handles tier-1 support questions via chat. Escalates to human for tier-2+
- **Health Score Agent** — monitors customer usage, flags at-risk accounts to human CS
- **Feedback Agent** — synthesizes customer feedback from support tickets, reviews, interviews into weekly product insights report

### 📊 Operations Agents
- **Finance Agent** — tracks MRR, ARR, churn, CAC, LTV. Generates weekly metrics dashboard
- **Legal Monitor Agent** — watches for regulatory changes (SEC, FINRA) relevant to RaiseOS. Flags to CEO
- **Competitive Intel Agent** — monitors Dynamo, Allvue, Juniper Square, iCapital for product updates, pricing changes, press releases
- **Fundraising Agent** — tracks VC/angel activity in fintech/private markets, identifies new investors to approach

### 🌅 The Daily Operating Rhythm
Every morning the CEO gets a briefing (like our morning brief, but for the company):
- MRR movement + key drivers
- Top 3 customer issues
- Sales pipeline movement
- Any competitive intelligence
- Recommended top 3 priorities for the day
- PRs waiting for review
- Outreach drafts waiting for approval

The CEO spends the morning reviewing agent outputs, approving or editing, making calls. The afternoon is for strategy and relationships.

## What This Means for Hiring

Traditional fintech company at $2M seed: 8-10 people
RaiseOS at $2M seed: 3 people + agent fleet

The 3 people should be extraordinary. Pay them extremely well. Give them meaningful equity (3-5% each). Attract believers, not employees.

Never hire for a function that an agent can do.
Hire only when a human is genuinely irreplaceable for that function.
