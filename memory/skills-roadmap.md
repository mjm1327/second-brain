## 🤖 Trading Bot — To Discuss

### The Vision
Build an AI-powered crypto trading bot where:
- You give it a crypto wallet (read/write access via API)
- You define a trading strategy (or let AI generate one)
- It executes trades autonomously based on signals
- Reports back via Telegram with every action and P&L

### Strategy Options to Explore
1. **Momentum/trend following** — buy when BTC breaks above 20-day MA, sell when it breaks below
2. **Narrative trading** — Merlin spots trending narratives on X → buys relevant tokens before they pump
3. **Mean reversion** — buy dips, sell rips on high-liquidity pairs
4. **AI signal bot** — Merlin analyzes news + X sentiment → generates buy/sell signals for your review
5. **Hyperliquid perps** — Automated leveraged trading on HYPE/BTC/SOL using Hyperliquid API

### Exchanges with Good APIs
- **Coinbase Advanced (REST + WebSocket)** — Best for US users, BTC/SOL/etc
- **Hyperliquid** — Decentralized perps, great API, fits your HYPE interest
- **Kraken** — You watch KRKN, they have solid API
- **Binance** — Most liquidity but US restrictions

### What We Need to Build
- Exchange API connection (read balances, place orders, track positions)
- Strategy engine (rules or AI-generated signals)
- Risk management (max position size, stop loss, daily loss limit)
- Telegram reporting (every trade, daily P&L summary)
- Paper trading mode first (simulate without real money)

### Important Notes
- **Start with paper trading** — test strategy for 2-4 weeks before real money
- **Risk management is #1** — never risk more than you can lose
- **Regulatory note** — automated trading is legal for personal accounts in the US
